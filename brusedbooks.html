About

Brused Books is where the used books go. Located in Pullman, WA., Brused Books has been serving the community since 1981.

Come inside and enjoy your favorite mystery. Interested in classics? We have those too. Wondering if you can find a textbook, it's possible. With books in every direction, you're sure to find something you love. Our shelves are flowing with fiction, non-fiction, Children's books, and more. Come see what makes us great.
